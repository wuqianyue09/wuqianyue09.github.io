## About Me

I am currently a master's student in Statistics at Beijing Normal University, with a bachelor's degree in Statistics from Renmin University of China.

My research interests lie in the **theoretical foundations of machine learning**, with a particular focus on:

- Transformer architectures  
- Attention mechanisms  
- Kernel methods and approximation  

I aim to pursue a PhD in **Machine Learning / Statistics**, focusing on theory-driven research.
